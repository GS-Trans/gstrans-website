## FAQ

### How do I book a transport?
Download the GS Trans customer app from Google Play Store, create an account, select pickup and drop locations, choose vehicle type, and confirm booking. A rider will be assigned within minutes.

### What areas do you serve?
We currently serve 20+ cities across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, including Khammam, Hyderabad, Vijayawada, Warangal, and surrounding districts.

### How is pricing calculated?
Pricing is based on distance, vehicle type, and any additional services. You'll see the estimated price before confirming your booking. No hidden charges.

### Can I track my shipment?
Yes! Real-time GPS tracking is available in the customer app. You can see your rider's location and estimated arrival time.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery. Business customers can opt for monthly billing.

### What if my booking is cancelled?
If cancelled within 5 minutes of booking, there's no charge. After that, a small cancellation fee applies. Full refund if rider cancels.

### Do you provide insurance?
Yes, all shipments are covered with basic insurance. Additional coverage can be purchased for high-value items.

### How do I become a GS Trans rider?
Download the GS Trans Rider app, complete registration with your documents (driving license, RC, Aadhaar), pass verification, and start accepting bookings.

### What vehicle types are available?
We offer bikes, auto-rickshaws, tempos, mini-trucks, and full-size trucks depending on your cargo size and requirements.

### Is customer support available 24/7?
Yes, our support team is available 24/7 via in-app chat, phone, and email to assist with any queries or issues.
